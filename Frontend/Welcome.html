Welcome to Quizzio!

Welcome to Quizzio – Your Ultimate Study Companion! 🎓📚 Quizzio is an interactive quiz and flashcard platform designed to help you learn, revise, and test your knowledge efficiently. Whether you’re studying for an exam, practicing new concepts, or just want to challenge yourself, Quizzio provides the perfect tools to enhance your learning experience. ✨ How to Use Quizzio 📌 Getting Started 1. Open Quizzio – When you land on the homepage, you will be greeted with a simple and intuitive interface. 2. Navigate Easily – The bottom navigation bar helps you quickly switch between the Home, Library, and Create sections. 3. Access Your Profile – In the top right corner, you’ll see your username and profile settings, where you can manage your account. 🚀 Features & How to Use Them 1️⃣ Create Your Own Flashcards • Click on the “Create” tab from the bottom navigation bar. • Fill in the question and answer fields to create a new flashcard. • Click “Save” to store the flashcard in your library. • Use flashcards for quick revision and self-testing. 2️⃣ Explore & Manage Your Flashcards • Go to the “Library” tab to access all your saved flashcard sets. • Click on a flashcard set to start reviewing. • Mark difficult flashcards for easy access later. • Delete flashcards you no longer need. 3️⃣ Test Yourself with Quizzes • Select a flashcard set from the library to generate a quiz. • Answer multiple-choice or short-answer questions. • Get instant feedback on your answers. • Track your progress to see how well you’re doing. 4️⃣ Customize Your Learning • In your Profile section, you can edit your username, email, and password. • Bookmark flashcards and quizzes for later review. • Challenge yourself with different flashcard categories.
